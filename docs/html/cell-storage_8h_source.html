13 /// @file
14 /// @brief Defines the storage data structure for the Cell%s.
15 ///
16 /// Implementation:
17 /// - CellBlocks store NxN block of cells
18 /// - Universe is made up of a grid of CellBlocks
19 /// - All cells in a CellBlock are initialised
20 /// - CellBlocks are stored on the heap, and accessed via a hashmap on the block position.
21 /// - ie.: block_pos = cell.pos / block_size
22 /// - On simulation, each cell block is simulated as a whole
23 /// - To iterate over all CellBlock%s / Cell%s just loop through the hashmap.
24 /// - Possible optimisation: CellBlocks store pointers to neighbours for quick access to border cell
25 /// states.
